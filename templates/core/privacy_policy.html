Privacy Policy

Last updated: September 11, 2025

Introduction

The Kenya National Missing Persons Database (KNMPDB) is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our platform to report or search for missing persons.

Information We Collect

Personal Information
  • Name and contact information when you register an account
  • Details about missing persons (name, age, description, photos, last known location)
  • Contact information for reporting parties
  • Communications between users and our platform
Technical Information
  • IP address and browser information
  • Usage data and analytics
  • Cookies and similar tracking technologies

How We Use Your Information

  • To facilitate the search and identification of missing persons
  • To maintain and improve our database services
  • To communicate with users about their reports and inquiries
  • To ensure the security and integrity of our platform
  • To comply with legal obligations and assist law enforcement

Information Sharing

We may share information in the following circumstances:

  • Law Enforcement: We cooperate with police and other authorities in missing person investigations
  • Public Safety: Missing person information is made publicly available to aid in searches
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights and safety
  • Service Providers: With trusted third parties who assist in operating our platform

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

Your Rights

  • Access and review your personal information
  • Request corrections to inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your information (subject to legal requirements)
  • Opt-out of non-essential communications

Children's Privacy

Our service is intended for users 18 years and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18 without parental consent.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us:

  • Email: privacy@knmpdb.org
  • Phone: +254 700 000 000
Note: This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. We will notify users of any significant changes through our platform.
